Sales forecasting & Media Impact Analysis.¶

Abstract¶

This project was made for the challenge promoted by Fater S.p.A. about forecasting sales with time series data, using Machine Learning and other modern methods. This Challenge was addressed to the students of the Master of Science on Data Science at University Federico II of Naples. The challenge has two main goals: First, build a model to obtain the best Tampax sales volume estimate by week and by product Stock Keeping Unit for the total Italian market for the semester April 2019 to September 2019. Second, build a model to understand the impact of seasonality, media and promos on Tampax Sell Out Volumes. To achieve the best possible results, three different methods were applied: Vector Autoregression, Sarimax and Long Short-Term Memory. Each of these methods yielded different results.

Introduction¶

Tampons are a feminine hygiene product designed to absorb the menstrual flow together with the pads and menstrual cups. In Italy, Tampax, a brand owned by Fater S.p.A., owns 68% of the value share. The company is the leader in Italy for hygiene and home products, often launching social initiatives and campaigns like “#decidoio”, aiming to remove taboos related to using tampons. The company also teams with female athletes and professionists to promote the freedom of choice of women. As of now, the Italian market of tampons is divided in two segments: with applicator, more hygienic and easier to use and without applicator, practical to bring as spare. For the Tampax brand, there are several products on the market: Blue Box, with a paper applicator, the most successful one according to the datasets, Compak, with a compact applicator, Tampax&Go, designed for the comfort and the easy application, Camomile, without applicator and Cotone, 100% cotton made. These products are strictly tied to the datasets given to us, as they all vary in terms in sales, popularity, promotions and innovation. Each product showed different behaviors when analyzed with our models.

Datasets¶

Historical Series Tampons:¶

The core dataset, containing time series of sales from January 2017 to March 2019. The dataset contains: Data Rif: Date in which the product was sold to the distributor. Category: Aggregation of segments that groups the products that cover the same need. Segment: Aggregation of SKU (Stock Keeping Unit) based on product distinctive features. Product: Single Stock Keeping Unit Customer: Italian distributor who sells our product to the final consumer. Standard Units: Daily volumes sold from Fater to the distributors (sell-in). Regione, Provincia, Channel: Geographical informations about the distributors.

About¶

There are three folders, one for each model, inside each there is everything required to run the code, with detailed descriptions of each step.

SARIMAX & LSTM¶

After running the code, select the how many weeks to forecast (22 for the max amount), then select the desired segment and product listed. Only for LSTM instead, specify the number of times to repeat the forecast; this helps stabilize the model.
